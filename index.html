Loc X. Nguyen

Semantic communications · DeepJSCC · 6G wireless networks

I am a Research Professor at Kyung Hee University (KHU), South Korea, building intelligent, communication-efficient systems for next-generation (6G) wireless networks.

I received my B.Eng. from Ho Chi Minh University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam, and my Ph.D. from Kyung Hee University in 2025. My work centers on semantic communications and deep joint source–channel coding — spanning multi-user and federated semantic communication, non-terrestrial networks (6G LEO satellites), and generative AI and large language models for wireless systems.
